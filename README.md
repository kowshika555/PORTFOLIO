**Home Page:** A welcoming section introducing myself with an animated landing page.

**Contact Form:** A simple contact form that allows users to reach out to me for potential opportunities or collaborations.

Feel free to browse through the different sections of the portfolio to get a sense of my work. You can click on the project links to view live demos or GitHub repositories for the projects.
